Our Story · قصتنا

Born in the Médina,
Flavoured by History

Dar Carthage was born in 2003 in the heart of Tunis's ancient Médina — a city where Phoenician merchants once traded spices, where Romans feasted, and where today the scent of jasmine mingles with cumin in every alleyway.

Our chef, Mohamed Amine Gharbi, grew up watching his mother grind harissa by hand each morning in tunis. he trained in Lyon and Istanbul before returning to Tunisia to open a restaurant that celebrates what she calls "the most underrated cuisine in the Mediterranean."

We source our olive oil from the groves of Sfax, our saffron from Tataouine, and our fish from the boats of Bizerte — fresh every morning without exception.
